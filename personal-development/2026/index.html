    Health

    Doctor's Visits

    A primary care doctor is something that needs to be found this year. Kate's situation shows that datapoints on your health are important, primary care provider would be important this year.

    Fitness

    The name of the game this year is hypertrophy and strength. To do this workout cycles will be broken down into 8 week cycles, with the goal of 5 days in the gym and two recovery days. In the summer those recovery days will include a walk outside on the trails, and gym will go to 4 days a week to account for sprinting intervals on what would have been the 5th gym day.

    Recovery

    The goal this year is to create a managable schedule for sleep and maximize recovery as it relates to fitness. To achieve this, sleep needs to become more dialed in. No staying awake until almost 4 am anymore, the body just doesn't recover well enough anymore.

    The goal is to average 21 green recovery days a month, with an additional 5 days of above 50% yellow recovery. This will allow for some days of poor recovery, but still allow for alcohol consumption and some days of little rest
